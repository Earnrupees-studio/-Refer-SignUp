FAQ
How to participate in Refer and Earn program?

-Open the EaseMyTrip app/web and go to ‘My Account’.
-Click on the ‘Sign Up Now’ button and enter your details.
-Log in to your account and navigate to the ‘Refer & Earn’ page.
-Copy your referral link/code and share it via social media.
-Earn wallet money when your friend books travel using the app/web.

Can I avail of this offer on the Desktop version?

Yes, the referral code will work on the both web and app.

How to check my Wallet Money?

Log in to the EaseMyTrip app/web and tap on ‘My Wallet’ to check your balance.

If my friend is already registered with the EaseMyTrip app/web? Will the referral code work?

No. The referral code will work only if your friend is not registered with EaseMyTrip previously. Only then you will receive wallet money.

With how many people I can share referral code?

You can refer as many people you want. By becoming member and referring it to your friends, you can earn upto INR 2000.

What are the benefits of Refer and Earn in the EaseMyTrip app/web?

Through EaseMyTrip Refer and Earn program, you and your friend will save huge amount on your travel bookings.

Terms & Conditions

1) In case of a partial/full cancellation, the refer and earn event will be voided and cashback amount will be rolled back from the booking refund amount.

2) In the case of any misuse or abuse of the Event, EaseMyTrip reserves the right to deny the cashback to the customers.
